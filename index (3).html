CSV format guide
Any format works
The uploader accepts any CSV format — Freetrade exports, your own spreadsheet, or the template below. The only required columns are Symbol, Quantity, and Average Price.
Minimum format
Symbol,Name,Quantity,AvgPrice
AAPL,Apple Inc,10,178.50
NVDA,Nvidia,5,480.00
MSFT,Microsoft,8,390.00
Freetrade export also works
Title,Symbol,Type,Quantity,Price per Share
Apple Inc,AAPL,BUY,10,178.50
Nvidia,NVDA,BUY,5,480.00

Column names are flexible — the parser recognises variations like ticker, stock, shares, qty, avg cost, average price, unit price and more. Buys and sells are handled automatically — net quantity is calculated.
